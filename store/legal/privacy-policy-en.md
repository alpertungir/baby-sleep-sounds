# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 20, 2026  
**App:** Baby Sleep Sounds (Bebek Uyku Sesleri)  
**Developer:** Tngr Studio  
**Contact:** alperttungir@gmail.com

## Summary

Baby Sleep Sounds is a mobile app that plays calming sounds to help your baby sleep. We respect your privacy and do not collect personal data.

## Data we collect

The app **does not collect personal data**. We do not collect names, email addresses, location, advertising IDs, or usage analytics.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores the following **locally on your device** only:

| Data | Purpose | Location |
|------|---------|----------|
| Favorite sound list | Remember your favorites | Device (SharedPreferences) |
| Language preference | Remember your UI language | Device (SharedPreferences) |
| Downloaded sound files | Offline playback | Device cache |

This data is not sent to our servers and is not shared with third parties.

## Third-party services

| Service | Purpose | Data |
|---------|---------|------|
| Firebase Storage (Google) | Download sound files | No personal data; public sound file requests only |
| Google Play billing | Optional voluntary support purchases | Payment processed by Google; the app does not collect personal data |

The app does **not** use advertising, analytics, or social media SDKs.

## Children's privacy

The app is designed for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Internet access

Some sounds are downloaded from Firebase Storage on first play. Part of the white noise and Turkish lullabies are bundled in the app and work offline.

## Data security

We do not store personal profiles on servers. Favorites on your device are subject to your device security settings.

## Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not maintain a data inventory requiring access or deletion requests. You can remove favorites in the app or uninstall the app to clear local data.

## Changes

This policy may be updated. Material changes will be published on the store listing and on this page.
