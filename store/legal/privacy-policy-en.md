## Summary

Baby Sleep Sounds is a mobile app that plays calming sounds to help your baby sleep. We respect your privacy and do not collect personal data.

## Data we collect

The app **does not collect personal data**. We do not collect names, email addresses, location, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores the following **locally on your device** only:

| Data | Purpose | Location |
|------|---------|----------|
| Favorite sound list | Remember your favorites | Device (SharedPreferences) |
| Volume preference | Session-level playback setting | Device memory |

This data is not sent to our servers and is not shared with third parties.

## Third-party services

The current version does **not** use advertising, analytics, social media, or cloud sync SDKs.

## Children's privacy

The app is designed for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Internet access

The app does **not** require an internet connection for core features. Sound files are bundled inside the app.

## Data security

Because we do not collect data, there is no server-side storage. Local favorites are protected by your device's security settings.

## Your rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data inventory to access or delete. You can remove favorites in the app or uninstall the app to clear all local data.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be published on the app store listing and on this page.
